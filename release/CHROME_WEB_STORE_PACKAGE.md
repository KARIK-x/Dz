## Detailed Description

### Overview

Daraz Cashback is the transparent way to earn cashback on your Daraz.com.np purchases. Unlike other extensions, we show you exactly how much we earn and share a fair portion with you.

### How It Works

1. **Browse Daraz** - Shop normally on Daraz.com.np
2. **See Cashback Offer** - Widget appears on product pages showing "Earn Rs. 5 Cashback"
3. **Activate & Confirm** - Click to activate, review transparent disclosure
4. **Complete Purchase** - Buy through our affiliate link
5. **Earn Rs. 5** - Get Rs. 5 credited for qualifying purchases
6. **Request Payout** - Cash out via eSewa or Khalti at Rs. 100 minimum

### Why Choose Daraz Cashback?

**Transparent & Honest**
- We earn Rs. 30 commission per sale (we tell you upfront)
- You get Rs. 5 of that (fair share)
- No hidden fees or surprise deductions

**Privacy-First Design**
- Minimal permissions (only what's needed)
- Works exclusively on Daraz.com.np
- No personal data collection
- Anonymous user IDs only

**Simple & Fast**
- One-click activation
- Automatic tracking
- Real-time balance updates
- Quick payouts (7-14 days)

**Secure & Reliable**
- No account signup required
- Fraud detection for fair use
- Secure payment processing
- Open architecture

### Permissions Explained

This extension requests only essential permissions:

- **activeTab**: Detect Daraz product pages
- **storage**: Save your preferences and balance locally
- **scripting**: Inject cashback widget on Daraz pages
- **daraz.com.np**: Works only on Daraz Nepal (nowhere else)

We never access data from other websites or track your browsing.

### Requirements

- Chrome browser (version 88 or higher)
- Shopping on Daraz.com.np (Nepal)
- Valid eSewa or Khalti account for payouts
